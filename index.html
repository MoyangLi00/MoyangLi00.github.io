Dynamic Lighting in Neural Radiance Fields
Employ physical-based rendering to decompose the scene into illumunation, material, and geometry. We use the combination of point light source and environment map to model the dynamic lighting of the scene. Our method can remove the heavy shadow caused by uneven lighting and improve the quality of the scene reconstruction.
